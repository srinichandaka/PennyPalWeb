Privacy Policy

Your information, handled with care.

This Privacy Policy explains how PennyPal handles information when you use the app and this website.

Effective date

Effective date: 23 April 2026

Information PennyPal handles

PennyPal is designed to help users import statements, review transactions and track personal and household spending.

  • Imported financial statement data and transaction records that you choose to add
  • Household sharing information needed to support shared views between invited members
  • Basic support information if you contact PennyPal by email

How your information is used

  • To provide statement import, categorisation and spending insights
  • To support household sharing features between authorised household members
  • To respond to support requests and improve app reliability

Data sharing

PennyPal does not sell your personal information. Data is only shared as needed to provide app functionality, such as household sharing features you choose to enable.

Data retention

Information is retained only as long as needed to provide the app’s features, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

Data security

PennyPal takes reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your choices

  • You control what statements and transaction data you import
  • You control whether household sharing is enabled
  • You can contact PennyPal for support-related privacy questions

Children’s privacy

PennyPal is not intended for children under 13.
