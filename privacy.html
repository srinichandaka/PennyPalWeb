Privacy Policy

Your information, handled with care.

This Privacy Policy explains how PennyPal handles information collected through the app, support channels and website, including waitlist signups.

Effective date

Effective date: April 24, 2026

Information PennyPal may handle

  • Financial statement data and transaction records that you choose to import into the app
  • Household sharing information required to provide shared views between invited members
  • Basic support information if you contact PennyPal by email
  • Email addresses submitted through the waitlist or launch signup form

How information is used

  • To provide statement import, organisation and spending insight features
  • To support household sharing features between authorised household members
  • To respond to support requests and improve reliability
  • To send launch updates or product announcements when you join the waitlist

Legal and product basis

PennyPal uses information only to operate the app, communicate about the product, respond to support requests and meet applicable legal obligations.

Data sharing

PennyPal does not sell personal information. Information may be shared only when needed to deliver product functionality, such as household sharing features you choose to enable, or with service providers used to support the website, waitlist or support workflows.

Data retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, manage support, send requested launch updates, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

Data security

PennyPal takes reasonable measures to protect information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure. Users should avoid sending highly sensitive financial credentials by email.

Your choices

  • You control what statements and transaction data you import
  • You control whether household sharing is enabled
  • You can request support or privacy help by emailing PennyPal
  • You can unsubscribe from launch emails at any time by contacting PennyPal

Children's privacy

PennyPal is not intended for children under 13.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact pennypal99@gmail.com.

This policy applies to the PennyPal website and related app support flows published from the GitHub Pages site.
