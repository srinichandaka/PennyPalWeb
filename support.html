Help for imports, sharing and everything in between.

Need help with household sharing, statement imports or syncing? PennyPal support is here to help you get back to a clearer money picture.

Frequently asked questions

How does household sharing work?

PennyPal is built to help couples track money together. Shared household expenses can be viewed together while personal and household spending stay clearly separated.

What can I import into PennyPal?

PennyPal supports statement import workflows for supported bank files, including statement-based transaction imports used to build your spending view and insights.

Why do some expenses appear as personal and others as household?

PennyPal is designed to preserve clarity. Transactions can be organised by ownership so you can tell what belongs to one person and what belongs to the household.

What should I do if sync looks out of date?

Open PennyPal, check that both devices are online and use the in-app sync actions if available. If the problem continues, contact support with a short description of what you expected to see.

How can I get direct help?

Email support@pennypal.app and include:

  • Your device model and iOS version
  • What you were trying to do
  • Whether the issue relates to import, household sharing or syncing
  • Any screenshot or error message that helps explain the problem
